Who we are

NRG Retail is comprised of a team committed to creating a different kind of energy company direct to the customer. By leveraging emerging technologies and partnerships, NRG is working to become the thought leader in the energy space.

Our Philadelphia office is tackling the job of transforming how people think about, and purchase, energy. By disrupting the system of monolithic utilities which only have a one size fits all model, we envision a more nimble future where customers have meaningful energy choices. With choice we can deliver value to customers where none exists now.

Our people and systems connect our customers to the best mileage and points reward programs in the market. Currently this loyalty platform sets NRG apart, but it is just a step in the direction we are heading. Our exclusive and one-of-a-kind relationships with over a hundred partners allows us to custom tailor products that will drive innovation in the retail energy space. We are constantly working on new products and services and are looking for people to come join us to change retail energy.


Why work at NRG Retail?

Let's be honest here; like every company that went a thousand miles an hour for a couple years, there is legacy code. Our legacy code takes the form of a LAMP stack that powers sections of our corporate and marketing infrastructure as well as .NET platform that is our Ops and CRM. Like an old Buick, it's comfortable and gets everyone where they need to go, but there are miles on it and maintenance isn't worth it (or fun) anymore.

So why on earth would you want to work here? Because we have been given the high five to start core systems over again. We've introduced a new sales system using (the now coined) MEAN stack and a new loyalty-awarding platform in python with celery and flask. The legacy CRM is being decommissioned, and the LAMP stack is seeing a new future as a CMS with rich APIs for core Marketing needs. New core systems we must build for the future of NRG Retail, are lightweight decoupled (and API-driven) using Python, Rabbit/Celery, and AWS. Our work will power NRG Retail for the future.
